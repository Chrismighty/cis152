Our Story

At Pacific Trails, we believe in creating unforgettable experiences that connect our guests with nature. Established in 1995, our family-owned resort has welcomed thousands of adventurers seeking relaxation and rejuvenation in the breathtaking landscape of the Pacific Northwest. Our mission is to provide exceptional service, immersive experiences, and a deep respect for the natural beauty that surrounds us.

Our dedicated team is passionate about hiking, wellness, and sustainability. We strive to offer unique packages that cater to every guest’s needs, whether you’re looking for a peaceful retreat or an action-packed getaway. Join us at Pacific Trails, where every moment is an opportunity to explore, unwind, and create lasting memories.

Our Team

At Pacific Trails Resort, our team is the heart of our operations. Comprising passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds, we are committed to providing exceptional service and unforgettable experiences for our guests. Each member of our team brings unique skills and expertise, from our talented chefs who create delicious, locally sourced meals to our knowledgeable tour guides who share the wonders of the Pacific Northwest. With a shared love for the outdoors and a dedication to sustainability, we work together to ensure that every visitor feels welcome and at home. We believe in fostering a positive and supportive environment, empowering our staff to excel and deliver the highest level of hospitality. Join us as we continue to create memories that last a lifetime!
